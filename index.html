Now that the table shows the most popular consoles from every generation, let me explain about what the consoles were.

1st generation: Magnavox Odyssey

The most popular first generation console was the magnavox Odyssey. This console became the first home console. The conosole had a bunch of games, but some of them are Alpine Skiing, Dynasty, and Football.

2nd generation: Fairchild video entertainment system

The Fairchild video entertainment system was a gaming console based on a micropresser and to use ROM cartridges instead of having built in. Some of the games on this console were The videocart games (1-21), Demo-cart-1, and hockey.

3rd generation:Famicom/NES

The Nintendo Etnertainment System (NES) is an 8-bit thid generation home gaming console made by nintendo. The games on this console are Super Mario Bros 3, Dr. Mario, the old mario games, and the Legend of Zelda.

4th generation:Mega Drive/Genesis

The Sega Genesis mini (Known as the mega drive) is a 16-bit fourth-generation home video game cnosole made by sega. The games on this console are the sonic the hedgehog games, Mortal Kombat II, and NHL '94.

5th generation:Nintendo 64

The nintendo 64 (N64) is a home gaming console made by Nintendo. The games on the N64 are Super Mario 64, Super Smash bros, and Paper Mario.

6th generation:Playstation 2 (PS2)

The Playstation 2 (PS2) is a home gaming console made by Sony Computer Entertainment. Some of the games on the PS2 are GTA vice city, GTA san andreas, and GTA III.

7th generation:Nintendo Wii

The nintendo wii is another home console made by nintendo, but this console was the first console to directly support internet connectivity (online gaming). Some of the games on the nintendo wii are Wii sports, Mario Kart Wii, and New Super Mario Bros.

8th generation:Xbox One

The Xbox One was a home gaming console created by microsoft. The games that were popular on this console were Halo 5, Halo: the master cheif collection, The Elder Scrolls V skyrim, and many more.

9th generation:Xbox Seires X|S

Now we wrap this presentation up with the newest generation of console: the xbox series X|S which were made by Microsoft. Some of the most popular games on this console are Halo Infinite, Gears 5, Elden Ring, Assassins Creed Valhalla, and many more.
